Hello World,
I'm Georg, a 20 year old, first year computing student originally from Berlin - Germany. As you would expect, I have a passion for technology and software, more concretely for iOS Development which I have done for almost 2 years now. One of the coolest things of coming to Imperial are the sheer number of events that are run by DocSoc, i.e. bar nights, the IC Hack Hackathon, you name it.
Consequently, I decided that I wanted to contribute more to this superduperawesome society as being the webmaster. Since social media and building up my own web presence are current interests of mine anyway, it seemed only logical to me to apply for this position.

So what would I do for you and the society? First of all, keep up the high quality work of the current webmaster's. Be it blogging about the most recent events, answering next year fresher's worried questions or simply ensuring that there is a general awareness of DocSoc's next ventures.
On top of that I've got some ideas of improving current affairs even more: Amomg these are: Branded DocSoc emails, instead of just plain texts; a google, iCal, etc. calendar subscription for all the events or a DocSoc newsletter about hot news in tech industry, maybe even with a special London focus.
That's it from me. Now I wan't you to make the next (recursive) step towards absolute greatness of DocSoc and vote for me as the next Webmaster.
